i ponder about the idea of a blog again.

between 18-23, i wrote a few. an 'outlet' between 18-21, a space to 'critically reflect' between 19-23, weekly 'intellectual updations' at 23 and a ridiculous 'everyday-microblog' at 24. the answer to why i want one more now eludes me. i write to find out.

i enjoy reading blogs of people i admire. work is often refined and polished, but blogs are more human. the person suddenly becomes discoverable & vulnerable.

as i begin writing, perhaps, i shall discover what this blog is about; and update this note then.
